Hi! I'm a third-year CS PhD student at Stanford University, advised by Chelsea Finn. My research is supported by a NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.

My goal is to build practical agents that can quickly solve new tasks by leveraging prior experience on related tasks. Toward this end, my research focuses on meta-reinforcement learning.

I spend most of my free time trying not to pull too hard on plastic or real rock (rock climbing). Reach out if you want to climb!
