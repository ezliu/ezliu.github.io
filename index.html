About

Hi! I'm a master's student studying Artificial Intelligence at Stanford University, advised by professor Percy Liang. I completed my undergraduate degree at Stanford University, as well, studying computer science, with a secondary major in mathematics.

My research interests are focused mainly on reinforcement learning. I'm particularly interested in applying reinforcement learning methods to healthcare, computer security, and game playing. In the past, I've worked on projects on reinforcement learning with demonstrations for web tasks and semantic parsing.

In my research, I've been fortunate enough to collaborate with Percy Liang, Emma Brunskill, Kelvin Guu, and Panupong (Ice) Pasupat.
