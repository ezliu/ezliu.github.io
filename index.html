About

Hi! I'm a Google Brain resident working on reinforcement learning. Next year, I'll be starting my PhD at Stanford University. Previously, I completed my master's and my undergraduate degrees at Stanford, advised by professor Percy Liang.

Broadly, my research interests are focused on reinforcement learning. In particular, I'm interested in hierarchical reinforcement learning, learning from demonstrations, and efficient exploration. I'm excited in the domains of healthcare, computer security, and game playing. In the past, I've worked on projects on reinforcement learning with demonstrations for web tasks and semantic parsing.

In my research, I've been fortunate enough to collaborate with Percy Liang, Emma Brunskill, Kelvin Guu, and Panupong (Ice) Pasupat.
